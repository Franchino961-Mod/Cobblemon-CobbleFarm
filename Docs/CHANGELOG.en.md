All notable changes to the **CobbleFarm** mod will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [1.0.0] - Initial Release
### Added
- **5 Farm Tiers**: Added Iron, Gold, Diamond, Emerald, and Netherite farm blocks to automate Pokémon drops.
- **Farm Ball**: A special item used to capture Pokémon and insert them into the farms.
- **Durability System**: Farm Balls have a maximum durability of 256 uses and will take damage based on the tier of the farm they are placed in. Higher tiers cause less damage.
- **Smart Output**: Farms automatically push generated loot into any valid inventory block (Chest, Hopper, etc.) placed directly below them.
- **Dynamic 3D Models**: Captured Pokémon are rendered dynamically inside the Farm Ball both in the inventory and while sitting in the farm block.
- **Loot Table Integration**: Drops are generated directly from the captured Pokémon's natural vanilla/Cobblemon loot tables.
